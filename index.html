Green Bean

How to Brew It Right

  1. Coarse grind, 1:15 coffee to water ratio.
  2. Pour water at 94°C, stir gently, cap the plunger.
  3. Steep for 4 minutes, then press down slowly.
  4. Serve immediately to avoid over-extraction.
